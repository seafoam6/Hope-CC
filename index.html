Our Services

Hope Christian Counseling offers a number of services to help you grow in your emotional and spiritual life

Counseling

"...if we walk in the Light as He Himself is in the Light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus His Son cleanses us from all sin." 1 John 1:7

Hope Christian Counseling sees counseling as an opportunity for believers to come together and work through their struggles. We combine contemporary psychological principles with scriptures and prayer to help our clients with depression, anxiety, ADHD and other mental health problems.

Marriage and Family Therapy

"Be completely humble and gentle; Be patient, bearing with one another in love. Make every effort to keep the unity of the spirit through the bond of peace."Ephesians 4:2-3

Through faith, fellowship, and prayer, Hope Christian Counseling can help your relationship with the people you care about the most. Call and ask how we can improve your marriage with Christian principles.

Substance Abuse Evaluation

I appeal to you therefore, brothers, by the mercies of God, to present your bodies as a living sacrifice, holy and acceptable to God, which is your spiritual worship.Romans 12:1

Hope Christian Counseling is internationally certified to perform substance abuse evaluations. Our system helps individuals develop a better understanding of their relationship with substances like alcohol and drugs. We offer treatments options for individuals who need help taming their addictions.
